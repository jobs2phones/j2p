Storeroom Attendant (Philadelphia, PA)

compensation: $13.50/hour
At the direction of the Site Manager , the successful candidate will be responsible for performing ongoing tasks associated with Storeroom Operations, such as (but not limited to) handling parts, receiving, stocking, issuing, cycle counting, interfacing with client end users, and delivery. Providing direct material support to the company's key customers, this position is a regular, frequent point of contact with the client and requires a strong, effective customer service capability.

Responsibilities:
Picking and/or issuing parts to customers in response to their needs. Issuing parts includes the completion of correct documentation to record the transaction, reacting to stock outs by notifying the buyer, and providing responsive customer service.
Receive parts into inventory, verifying pack list and material and entering transactions into SDI and/or client software systems.
Stock parts after receipt into inventory by placing material into correct storage location, following procedures for FIFO and maintaining lot integrity.
Accurate cycle counting of inventory to prescribed frequency
Customer interactions - Notify customer (requisitioner) after parts are received (one time orders or back-in-stock) as applicable.
Deliver parts as required by customer agreement or as directed by management, making sure to follow safety and material handling rules.
Communicate out of stock or special needs to help ensure that stock fill rates and lead times are optimized.
Following customer required procedures such as factory rules for building access, safety, parking, and administrative requirements (such as entering data, completion of records). Customer required procedures will be communicated to the Storeroom Clerk by his/her supervisor.
Perform other duties assigned

Qualifications:
High School Diploma/equivalent
Preferably minimum 6 month exp. in warehouse/storeroom operations.
Able and willing to work in an industrial environment; may be exposed to heat, noise, and dirt.
Must be able to lift weight unaided of up to 50 pounds; higher weights using equipment such as pallet jacks.
Must be a motivated self-starter with strong organizational skills and the ability to multi-task.
Preferred experience working with trades in a maintenance or repair environment.
Must be able to demonstrate excellent written and verbal communication skills.
Knowledge of MS Office software and familiarity with ERP software.
Basic math skills
Customer service experience.
May be required to work overtime.

**Please apply via email and share your most updated resume. Thank you!
