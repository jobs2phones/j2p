Warehouse Quality Control Inspector (Thorofare, NJ)

(google map) (yahoo map)

compensation: Salary TBD + Benefits
Position Overview:
The Quality Control Inspector's primary role is to inspect finished components and fixtures, complete all related inspection logs and reports, and communicate back to project managers any issues or corrective actions needed to resolve a QC problem in a timely manner.

Responsibilities:
-Perform final inspection on fixture inventory using shop drawings and agreed upon quality standards.
-Complete all QC documentation as it relates to daily inspection process (reports, logs, etc.)
-Perform inspection of product built at remote locations (as needed).
-Assist in the processing of Returned Goods from the field and issue appropriate reports.
-Assist QC Supervisor in the completion of the monthly Safety and QA reports.
-Communicate with Production team any issues found during the process of evaluating material.
-Photo document issues/defects and save pictures into the appropriate network location for product team reference.
-Work with receiving/warehouse team to plan and stage inventory for QC processing.
-Other duties as deemed appropriate by management.

Qualifications:
-High School Diploma or related work experience, Associates or Technical Degree preferred
-Travel to remote manufacturing facilities required as needed (approximately 10% of the time)
-Minimum 3-5 years Quality Control experience
-Manufacturing and/or retail fixture experience a plus
