Experienced Furniture Deliveryman (Yeadon)

compensation: Depends on skills and experience
Full time position available for an experienced furniture mover. Must have knowledge of how to put furniture together, how to navigate tight spaces, loading and unloading trucks, good customer service skills, drive a 16' box truck, lift heavy household goods, maintain and organize a warehouse, and wrap and carton goods for shipment.

Must be available Monday through Friday with an occasional Saturday. Located on Baltimore Avenue in Yeadon, PA, just over the Philadelphia line.

Must have drivers license with clear driving history (CDL not required) as well as Medical Card.
Must have experience driving trucks
US Citizen and clean background check

Please e-mail your resume and phone number and some information about yourself. No phone calls please.
